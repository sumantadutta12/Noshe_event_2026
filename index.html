About NOSHE

Why Attend?

If you look at the challenges we are facing right now, it is clear that standard compliance checklists aren't enough anymore. Managing Safety, Health, and the Environment (SHE) requires a completely different level of agility.

This year, under the theme "Stronger SHE for Building a Brighter Tomorrow," NOSHE 2026 isn't just about reviewing regulations. It's a hands-on look at how to build a workplace that can withstand economic shifts, regulatory changes, and a volatile climate. We are bringing together the people who are actually shaping the industry: policy makers, safety directors, environmental engineers, and corporate leadership from across the globe.

Here is why you need to be in the room:

01

Move from Reactive to Proactive Safety

Go beyond standard risk assessments. We are diving into how top organizations are building deep safety cultures that aim for "Zero Harm" by focusing on human values and behavioral psychology.

02

De-risk Your ESG Strategy

Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) targets are moving fast. You will get a clear look at how to build audit-ready, transparent compliance platforms that turn complex data into a real competitive advantage.

03

Demystify AI and Digital Tools

Skip the hype. This is a practical look at how predictive AI and digital innovations are actually being used on the ground to prevent incidents and monitor occupational health in real time.

04

Practical Climate Adaptation

Climate change isn't a future problem; it affects daily operations right now. Learn how leading companies are redesigning their emergency preparedness and adopting circular economy models to protect both their people and their bottom line.

05

Connect with the Right People

The biggest value often happens between sessions. You will be networking with peers and experts who face the exact same operational headaches you do, giving you a chance to share notes and bring back proven solutions to your team.

The landscape is changing rapidly. Don't wait for a major incident or a regulatory penalty to adapt. Join us at NOSHE 2026 to get the insights, tech strategies, and peer connections you need to build a safer, more resilient organization.

Attendee Profile

Who Should Attend?

NOSHE 2026 is designed for cross-functional leaders, specialists, and executives who are responsible for driving organizational resilience, safety culture, and environmental compliance. This year's summit is highly relevant for:

Executive & Operations Leadership

C-Suite Executives, Managing Directors, and Board Members looking to integrate ESG compliance directly into business continuity and corporate strategy.

EHS & Sustainability Professionals

Environment, Health, and Safety (EHS) Directors, Sustainability Officers, and Environmental Engineers dealing with climate adaptation, circular economy models, and source-to-sink management.

Digital Transformation & Tech Leaders

Chief Technology Officers (CTOs), IT Directors, and Innovation Managers focused on deploying AI, data analytics, and digital compliance platforms for workplace safety.

Occupational Health & Medical Professionals

Chief Medical Officers (CMOs), Occupational Health Physicians, Corporate Wellness Directors, Industrial Hygienists, and Ergonomists.

Risk & Crisis Managers

Risk Assessment Officers, Emergency Preparedness Specialists, and Disaster Management Professionals.

HR & Culture Executives

HR Directors and Organizational Culture Specialists working on behavioral safety, human values, and employee well-being.

Industry Enablers & Academics

OSH Trainers, Instructors, Policy Makers, Regulatory Authorities, and Manufacturers/Importers of advanced safety equipment.
