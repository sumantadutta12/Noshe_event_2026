About NOSHE

Why Attend?

NOSHE-2025 will bring together policy makers, thought leaders, industry titans, international experts, management representatives, Health, Safety, Security, Environment, Wellness & associated professionals from the wide spectrum of the national, regional & global industries.

Themed "Creating a Resilient Workplace: Sustainable Health, Safety and Environmental Strategies for a Changing Climate" NOSHE-2025 would underpin the need for integration & synergy of occupational health with other key aspects of business continuity planning, Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) including but not limited to safety, security, emergency preparedness, disaster management, ergonomics, hygiene, wellness, gender equality, organization culture, noise, and air pollution etc.

Who Should Attend

Attendee Profile

NOSHE-2025 is designed for Occupational Health Leaders, Managers & Executives including:

  • Occupational Health Physicians
  • Occupational Safety and Health professionals such as safety engineers safety technicians etc.
  • Public and private OSH organisations
  • Occupational Hygienists scientist
  • Occupational Health Consultants
  • Manufacturers and importers of safety materials and safety equipments
  • Instructors, trainers and teachers in the field of OSH education
  • Agriculture Scientist
  • Cardiology
  • Physiotherapists
  • Orthopaedics & many more......
