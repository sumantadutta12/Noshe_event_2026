About The Organiser

About The Organiser

NTPC Limited, India's largest integrated energy company, has been a cornerstone of the nation's economic growth since its establishment in 1975. With an installed capacity of over 77 GW, NTPC generates approximately 25% of India's total electricity, powering every fourth bulb in the country. The company focuses on delivering power that is economical, efficient and environmentally sustainable, offering a diversified energy mix that includes solar, wind, hydro, floating solar, gas, and coal.

NTPC is leading India’s energy transition with a target to increase non-fossil fuel-based capacity to 45-50% of its total portfolio by 2032, including 60 GW of renewable energy. Its efforts in renewable energy are evident with over 4 GW of operational renewable capacity and 20 GW in the pipeline.

The company has also pioneered green hydrogen initiatives, including the green hydrogen-PNG blending project at Kawas and a hydrogen fuel cell EV trial in Ladakh. Furthermore, NTPC is investing in carbon capture, green ammonia and pumped storage projects to reduce its carbon footprint. Globally, NTPC has expanded its presence with power projects in Bangladesh and Sri Lanka and advises several countries through the International Solar Alliance. Recognized for its excellence, NTPC ranks among the top Indian PSUs and global energy companies, continuing its commitment to national development, sustainability and social responsibility.

About The Scientific Partner

SOSHE (Society for Occupational Safety, Health & Environment Professionals)

Society for Occupational Safety, Health, and Environment Professionals (SOSHE) is registered under the SOCIETIES REGISTRATION ACT XXI of 1860, where our mission is to champion workplace safety, foster a culture of health, and promote environmental Sustainability.

As a leading non profit society in the field, SOSHE is dedicated to empowering professionals, businesses, and communities to create safer, healthier, and more environmentally responsible workplaces. The Society for Occupational Safety, Health, and Environmental Professionals (SOSHE) envisions a world where workplaces and communities prioritize the health, safety, and well-being of individuals and the environment.

The vision of SOSHE encompasses several key elements: Safe Workplaces, Healthy Workforce, Environmental Stewardship, Professional Excellence.
