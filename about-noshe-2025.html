About NOSHE 2025

The Conference

The Conference

The Governing Council of NOSHE 2025 is pleased to announce the inaugural edition of NOSHE 2025 scheduled to be held during 25th - 26th June 2025 at Power Management Institute (PMI), NTPC, Noida.

As India's flagship multidisciplinary & cross sector forum on Occupational Safety, Health & Environment NOSHE-2025 will bring together policy makers, thought leaders, industry titans, international experts, management representatives, Health, Safety, Security, Environment, Wellness & associated professionals from the wide spectrum of the national, regional & global industries.

Themed "Creating a Resilient Workplace: Sustainable Health, Safety and Environmental Strategies for a Changing Climate" NOSHE-2025 would underpin the need for integration & synergy of occupational health with other key aspects of business continuity planning, Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) including but not limited to safety, security, emergency preparedness, disaster management, ergonomics, hygiene, wellness, gender equality, organization culture, noise, and air pollution etc.

NOSHE 2025 brings together leaders and professionals from Occupational Safety, Health and Environment sectors.
